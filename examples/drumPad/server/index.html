The 3 sound buttons play their sounds every time they are clicked and are independent of the other buttons. The Chord button attemps to play 3 files at the same time. All the other buttons work together and independently of the Sound and Chord buttons. Exception: The volume buttons also apply to the Sound and Chord.. The "Last Audio Player" value gives the audio player used for the last Sound or Chord played. Values range from 1 to 9. Chord requires 3 audio players as three distinct files are played.
